Mission

Our goal is to inspire and nurture every single child in order to be a great human being to the society - one child at a time.

Vision

We aspire to see a future where all children can be given the opportunity to experience childhood development aroun a loving an caring social setup so that they may in the end become members of the society who can be relied upon in driving foward their communities.

Our Core Values and Strategic plan

Care Guidance Teamwork Talent Creativity
At Vision Academy, we belive that it is necessary to take good care of each student. A well taken care of child will grow up to be a person who can also shoulder the responsibility of caring for others. As an institution, Vision Academy is one of the best places for guiding young children as they step into the future. We forever know that a young mind can achieve anything that they desire as long as it is given proper guidance in order to set them rightly into the future. Vision Academy offers a team based schedule for most of the activities that the the students are engaged through. This kind of cirriculum is key in developing the ability of the child to work with other people comfortably within a group. Talent is important as it makes anyone more competitive. That is why at Vision Academy, children are taught how to identify their talents and nurture them. Creativity is at the heart of the institution, we are very passionate about the creativity of our young children. We believe that being creative is a very skill for any child still growing up. We believe in our young stars!
